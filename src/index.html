Instructions

Enter a combination of 0's and 1's below to perform basic logic gate operations.

The first two characters specify the operation:

01 will perform an AND operation.

10 will perform an OR operation.

11 will perform a NOT operation.

The final two characters specify the input.

For example, entering '0111' will perform an AND operation on 1 and 1.

The NOT operation is performed only on the third character,

and will ignore any fourth character.
