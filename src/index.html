Instructions

Use the punchcard below to perform basic logic gate operations.

Unchecked = 0

Checked = 1

The first two boxes (0 and 1) specify the operation:

01 will perform an AND operation.

10 will perform an OR operation.

11 will perform a NOT operation.

The second two boxes (2 and 3) specify the input.

For example, entering '0111' will perform an AND operation on 1 and 1.

The NOT operation is performed only on the input in the third box (position 2),

and will ignore any input in the final box.
